Saint Michael College Of Caraga

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC) is a private Catholic educational institution located in Nasipit, Agusan del Norte, Philippines. Committed to academic excellence and holistic development, SMCC provides quality education from basic education to higher education, equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for success. Guided by its core values of faith, service, and excellence, SMCC fosters a Christ-centered learning environment where students are encouraged to grow intellectually, morally, and socially. The institution offers a range of programs in business, education, information technology, and other disciplines, ensuring that students receive well-rounded training for their future careers. With modern facilities, dedicated faculty, and a strong emphasis on community engagement, SMCC continues to be a leading educational institution in Caraga, shaping students into competent professionals and responsible citizens.

Saint Michael College Of Caraga/About Us

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC) is a private Roman Catholic institution located in Nasipit, Agusan del Norte, Philippines. Established in 1948 by the Missionaries of the Sacred Heart (MSC) fathers, it operates under the Roman Catholic Diocese of Butuan.


Academic Programs:
SMCC offers a comprehensive range of educational programs across various levels:

Elementary Department: Preschool to Grade 6.

Junior High School Department: Grades 7 to 10.

Senior High School Department: Grades 11 and 12, with strands including General Academic Strand (GAS), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Science and Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Accounting, Business, and Management (ABM), Maritime, Home Economics, and Information Technology.

Colleges:
College of Tourism Hospitality Business and Management College of Teacher Education College of Arts and Sciences College of Computing and Information Sciences College of Criminal Justice Education Technical Department


Facilities and Initiatives:
In 2017, SMCC launched its own FM radio station, DXSM 89.5 Kabayaning Michaelinian FM, to promote Catholic education and teachings within the community. The college also publishes the "Michaelinian Mirror," an official publication that provides updates on the latest news and events within the school.


Vision and Mission:
SMCC envisions becoming a university by 2035, emphasizing spiritual formation and excellence in teaching, service, and research. The institution is committed to providing a learning culture that ensures students have transformative and rewarding experiences, engaging in dynamic research, and fostering community partnerships for spiritual and social development.

Saint Michael College Of Caraga/Services

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC) offers a variety of services to support the academic and personal development of its students:

Student Affairs and Services:
The Office of Student Affairs and Services (OSAS) is dedicated to promoting awareness and advocacy on important matters related to student life. They organize programs and activities designed to assist students in achieving their academic and personal goals.

Learning Resource Center:
SMCC's Learning Resource Center (LRC) provides access to a wide range of educational materials to support students' research and learning needs. The LRC aims to be a comprehensive library and information environment that underpins the institution's educational, research, and extension endeavors.

Student Portal:
The college offers an online Student Portal where students can access academic information, course materials, and other resources essential for their studies. Guidance on how to access and utilize the portal is available to ensure students can effectively navigate the platform.
